Q&A

What are the main differences between Web 1,2 and 3?

Web 1.0

A limited interaction with the user. Mostly used in reading sites with no option to add or post new data from the user.

Web 2.0

More options are added and the interaction is greater, allowing users to post their share their own information and share it with others easily.

Web 3.0

Websites and the systems behind them become more intelligent, and able to interpert information like humans. The computers adapt themselves to the user's personal perferences.

Examples:

Search for "Easiest ways to get to space".
Web 1.0 will mostly provide you with plain text sites giving you lots of reading materials.
Web 2.0 will give you links to various sites conatining the information you are looking for, inclding sites where you can share your knowledge with others and maybe even invite them on a cheap trans-galactic trip to Mars.
Web 3.0 will "understand" what you are looking for, and try to better personalize the browsing experienece based on machine learning techniques and Artificial Intelligence.
